🌟 Hidden Gems

A new way to discover those secret spots you’d never find in a travel guide.

💡 What’s the Idea?

Hidden Gems will be a social-style feature where users can share those unforgettable, under-the-radar places they stumble upon — a beautifully lit alley, a peaceful corner of a park, or a coffee shop with a vibe worth remembering.

✨ What You’ll Be Able to Do

  • 📸 Post your own Hidden Gem with photos and a story
  • ⭐ Rate and comment on places shared by others
  • ❤️ Add gems to your personal wishlist
  • 🧭 Browse by mood, location, or categories

This feature is in the works and will be released in a future phase of the app. Stay tuned!
